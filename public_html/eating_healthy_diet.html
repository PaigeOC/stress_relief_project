Healthy Eating

Since stress negatively affects blood pressure and blood flow, a healthy diet can help improve blood flow in the body. Cortisol, also known as the stress hormone, helps reduce inflammation, regulates sleep cycle, and controls blood pressure. Intaking foods with low fat and high protein allows the body to balance our stress levels.

Some foods that can help improve blood flow include omega-3s, vitamin B, and magnesium. Food is very powerful.

Eating a wide variety of healthy foods helps to keep you in good health and to protect against chronic disease. Eating a well-balanced diet means eating a variety of foods from each of the five food groups daily, in the recommended amounts. It is also important to choose a variety of foods from within each food group. Takeaway foods, cakes, biscuits and soft drinks are examples of foods usually high in saturated fat, added salt or added sugars. They should be considered as extras to your usual diet and only eaten occasionally and in small amounts. Daily food serves are different for children, teenagers, women and men. Food Options List
